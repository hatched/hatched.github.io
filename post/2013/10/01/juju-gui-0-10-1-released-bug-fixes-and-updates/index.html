Juju GUI 0.10.1 Released - Bug fixes and updates

1 October 2013

About a week after releasing the new service inspector in the Juju GUI we are following it up with another quick release which consists primarily of bug fixes.

The largest of those fixes are: - Changing settings did not work in Juju Core. (Fixed) - The GUI was unable to deploy charms without config options. (Fixed) - Remove unit button did not work in Juju Core. (Fixed) - Plus a number of additional fixes. You can read the full changelog here. Additional information on Juju can be found on https://jujucharms.com or join us in #juju on irc.freenode.net.
